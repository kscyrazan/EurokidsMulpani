About EuroKids Mulpani

A Place Where Children Thrive

EuroKids Mulpani is a proud branch of the renowned EuroKids International network — one of South Asia's most trusted early childhood education brands, serving over a million families. EuroKids Mulpani brings world-class pre-school education to the doorsteps of families in Mulpani, Kathmandu.

We follow the EuroKids ECEAP (Early Childhood Education and Activity Programme) — a research-backed curriculum that nurtures intellectual, emotional, physical, and social development in children aged 2 to 6.5 years.

Our warm, colourful campus is designed from the ground up to be a safe haven for little learners. With smart classrooms, an outdoor playground, and dedicated activity spaces, every corner of our school sparks imagination and joy.

About the Brand

EuroKids International – The Network Behind Us

EuroKids International is one of the largest and most respected chains of pre-schools in South Asia. With thousands of centres across India and now expanding in Nepal, EuroKids is synonymous with quality early childhood education. The brand's ECEAP curriculum has been recognised and praised by educators globally for its child-centric, activity-based approach.

As the Mulpani branch, we carry forward this legacy with local warmth, dedicated educators, and a deep commitment to the children and families of Kathmandu's growing Mulpani community.
